# MentalExtension (SafeGuard)
"Built in 48 hours at Mental Health AI Hackathon 2024 — 1st place"
> AI-powered browser extension that reduces online hate speech and cyberbullying by filtering harmful comments on social media.

## Background

Hate speech and cyberbullying have become significant contributors to the growing mental health crisis in today's digital society.

According to a **2023 UNESCO & IPSOS** survey:

- **67%** (2 out of 3) of internet users have encountered hate speech or violent language online.
- Among users **under 35 years old**, the number rises to **74%**.

Decades of psychological research have also shown that exposure to hate speech can:

- Cause long-term emotional and psychological harm to victims.
- Increase hostility and prejudice among people who witness these messages.
- Create a more toxic and unhealthy online environment.

## Our Solution

**SafeGuard** is an AI-powered browser extension designed to create a safer and more positive online experience.

Instead of simply blocking profanity, SafeGuard understands the intent behind comments and classifies them into categories such as:

- 🚫 Hate Speech
- ⚠️ Toxic or Offensive Language
- 💬 Constructive Criticism
- ✅ Positive & Supportive Comments

Users can choose to automatically **hide**, **remove**, or **display** comments based on these classifications.

## Features

- 🤖 AI-powered comment analysis
- 🧠 Distinguishes hate speech from constructive criticism
- 💬 Real-time filtering on social media platforms
- 🌍 Creates a healthier online experience
- 💻 Cross-platform support (PC, Tablet, Mobile)
- 🦊 Browser support including Firefox and Safari

## How it Works

1. Detect comments on a webpage.
2. Analyze each comment using an AI model.
3. Classify the comment by sentiment and toxicity.
4. Hide or display comments according to the user's preferences.

## Goal

Our goal is not to censor opinions, but to help users reduce exposure to harmful online content while preserving constructive discussion and healthy conversations.
